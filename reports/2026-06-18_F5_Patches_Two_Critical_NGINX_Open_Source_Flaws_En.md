## Executive Summary

F5 released patches for two critical NGINX Open Source vulnerabilities (CVE-2026-42530 and CVE-2026-42055) that enable remote code execution under specific conditions. The flaws require ASLR bypass or specific configuration settings. Affected versions across NGINX products were patched, with mitigations including disabling HTTP/3 and adjusting configuration directives.
